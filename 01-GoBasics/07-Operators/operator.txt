Logical Operators: 

    ! (Not)
    || (Or)
    && (And)

Bitwise Operators:

    & (bitwise and)
    | (bitwise or)
    ^ (bitwise xor)
    &^ (bitwise, and not)
    << (left shift)
    >> (right shift)


Comparison Operators:

    == (equal)
    != (not equal)
    <  (less than)
    <= (less than or equal)
    >  (greater than)
    >= (greater than or equal)
